Meet Bob Nyanja

“I'm sure anyone likes a good crime, provided they are not the victim.” ...

Story Craft in Cinematography

Bob is an accomplished all round versatile film professional with a proven ability to create, develop, shoot and produce the full gamut of ,film and Television Productions. He has created, self financed, and produced two award winning local features and directed two other with local producers. He has the full academic training and credentials in the film Business and has through 25 year experience worked on several different genres of film and television production, from the simple management level all the way to the director producer positions. ...He has successfully, managed a production house for 23 years, under which he has made films for local corporate organizations like Unilever, The Coca Cola Company, Procter and Gumble and Safaricom amongst many others. Internationally Bob has made films for Comic Relief, Unicef, Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation, BBC, ITV, China Global Television, ARD Germany and many others.. The production house has also serviced several international film and television productions.
In leadership, Bob has served as Chairman of the Kenya Film and Television Professional association for four years, Served in the government task force that led to the setting up of the Kenya Film Commission, Served as a trustee of the Kenya International film Festival for six year. His contribution to the arts earned him the presidential Award of Order of the Grand Warrior of Kenya. Bob is widely travelled within the African continent and has filmed in 17 countries across the continent.
